Ask for an urgent GP appointment if you have:

  • pain in your sides or lower back
  • a very high temperature or you feel hot and shivery
  • felt sick or been sick
  • diarrhoea

It could be a kidney infection. This can be serious if not treated.

If you cannot see a GP, go to 111.nhs.uk or call 111 to find out the right place to get help.
